Affine (transformation):

A geometric operation used in font rendering to scale, rotate, shear,
or translate text while preserving straight lines and
parallelism. Affine transformations are applied to the vector outlines
of glyphs, enabling high-quality, resolution-independent
rendering. When applied at the vector level, they maintain typographic
integrity; when applied at the bitmap level, they degrade quality due
to pixel interpolation.
